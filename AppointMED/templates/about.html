Frequently Asked Questions

  • What is MedEasy?

    MedEasy is a web application that allows you to schedule appointments with your doctor. You can also view your appointments and see your doctor's availability.


  • How do I schedule an appointment?

    To schedule an appointment, you don't need to sign up. Just click on a doctor which fits your needs and choose a date and time that works for you.

  • How do I view my doctor's availability?

    Clik on the "Home" tab and choose a doctor. After that you can select a date you would like to view availability for.
